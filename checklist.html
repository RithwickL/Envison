Essential Tips for FGLI Students

  • Start Early: Begin your application process as early as possible to avoid last-minute stress.
  • Research Financial Aid: Look into scholarships, grants, and work-study opportunities to ease financial burdens.
  • Seek Support: Reach out to mentors, teachers, and community organizations for guidance.
  • Stay Organized: Keep track of deadlines, required documents, and application statuses.
  • Personal Statement: Highlight your unique experiences and challenges to make your application stand out.
  • Letters of Recommendation: Ask for letters of recommendation early and provide your recommenders with sufficient time.
  • Prepare for Interviews: Practice common interview questions and present yourself confidently.
  • Double-Check Applications: Review your applications for any errors before submitting.

Example Checklist

  • Request transcripts
  • Complete FAFSA
  • Write personal statement
